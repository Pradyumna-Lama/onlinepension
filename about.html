ABOUT

The website named "Online Pension Service" provides services to the pensioners regrading the updation of the life certificate in the course every six months.

All the central and state goverment servants from any department can register to the website with their personal email id to update their pension book biennially every year.The website also introduces with the E-bank where the intrested people can open an account and transfer their pension salary directly to the bank.

The E-bank comes with many easy services and provide better facility.The website also consists of a pension calculator for the people to calculate their pension salary.
